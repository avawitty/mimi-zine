# Contributing  
Thank you for your interest in contributing to Mimi Zine! We welcome submissions of poems, essays, imagery, and other creative works. Please follow these guidelines:  
- Ensure your work is original and aligns with the zine's concept and structure.  
- Format submissions according to the templates provided in the repository.  
- Submit content via pull request and include a brief description of your piece.  
- Respect publishing cadence and deadlines.  
We appreciate your creativity and collaboration!
